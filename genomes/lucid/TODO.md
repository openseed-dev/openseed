# lucid genome

Combine dreamer (explicit memory) and wonders (subconscious retrieval) into a single genome.

## The idea

A creature with both memory systems:
- **Dreamer's explicit memory**: observations, rules, consolidation, dreams, self-evaluation, fatigue
- **Wonders' subconscious**: hypothesis-driven retrieval running in the background, surfacing relevant past experience the creature didn't ask for

The two systems are complementary. Explicit memory handles deterministic state (what do I know, what are my rules). The subconscious handles associative recall (what past experience is relevant right now that I wouldn't think to look for).
